What to Have Ready

You do not need everything before submitting. These are the records that make migration faster and cleaner.

Resident list Names, phone numbers, emails, unit labels, rent, due day, deposits held, last month rent, and open balances.
Rent roll or payment ledger Current month ledger plus any prior months you want reflected in reports or resident payment history.
Accounting records Bank exports, accounting software exports, invoices, receipts, debt service, utilities, and expense categories.
Tenant files Signed leases, move-in forms, emergency contacts, inspection notes, scanned packets, and insurance records.
Property settings Shared utilities, tenant-paid utility vendors, late fee rules, application fees, screening needs, listings, and owner/landlord users.
Report priorities Which reports matter first: rent roll, T-12, NOI, cash flow, valuation support, vendor expense, deposits, or custom reports.
