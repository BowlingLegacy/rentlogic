Painted Lady Inn Room Application

A stability-focused, drug- and alcohol-free shared housing community.

The Painted Lady Inn is a drug- and alcohol-free home for people who need safe, stable housing, including people at risk of homelessness, people rebuilding from addiction, and people seeking a respectful community environment.

House rules are required for the safety and stability of all residents. Our primary rule is Unconditional Positive Regard for others. These questions help us understand housing needs, support needs, and whether this community is a good fit.
